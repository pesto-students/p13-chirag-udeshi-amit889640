Varanasi

Location

Varanasi is situated in the middle of the Uttar Pradesh.

About City

This is the oldest city known in the history a holy city know for the hindu devotee

It is the city of temple and river Ganga

Best know for the temple Kashi Vishwanath and Sankat Mochan Mandir

This is also famous for Gautam Budh

Things To Do :- Visit the following places

  • Ghats of river Ganga
  • BHU university
  • Sarnath
  • Water falls of Lakhaniya dari

Weather

It has all the season and the climate is moderate sometime cloudy some time sun shine
